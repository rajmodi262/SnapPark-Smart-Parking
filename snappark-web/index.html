Core Features

Everything a Modern
Parking System Needs

Built from the ground up with enterprise-grade security, real-time analytics, and a relentless focus on zero-friction user experience.

📱

App-Less QR Access

No downloads, no sign-ups. Users scan a dynamic QR code and instantly access the full parking interface as a lightweight PWA — works on any phone, any browser.

Zero Friction
⭐

AI Slot Recommendation

Our scoring algorithm analyzes entry point proximity, historical demand heatmaps, and vehicle type compatibility to recommend the mathematically optimal parking slot.

Machine Learning
⚡

Dynamic Surge Pricing

Uber-style algorithmic yield management. Rates auto-adjust based on real-time occupancy percentage and time-of-day demand curves to maximize lot revenue.

Real-Time
🔐

Cryptographic 2FA Exit

Every session generates a unique 6-digit PIN bound to the user's device. The physical gate won't open without it — making vehicle theft inside the lot impossible.

Anti-Theft
🗺️

3D Digital Twin

An interactive isometric 3D grid renders the entire parking lot in real-time. Includes a booking-frequency heatmap mode for management analytics and spatial optimization.

Visualization
🤖

Autonomous Enforcement

A background LockSweeper thread acts as a digital police officer — detecting abandoned bookings, revoking stale locks, and auto-applying fines to the database.

User Journey

Park in 4 Steps.
No App Required.

The entire flow — from arrival to exit — takes under 90 seconds and requires zero prior setup from the driver.

📷

Scan QR

Drive up to the Kiosk. Point your phone camera at the screen. The parking app loads instantly in your browser.

🗺️

Pick a Slot

See the full lot in a live 3D grid. Our AI recommends the best spot based on proximity and demand analytics.

🔑

Get Your PIN

Confirm your booking and receive a cryptographic 6-digit session PIN. This is your anti-theft key for exit.

💳

Pay & Exit

When leaving, scan the Exit QR. See your dynamic bill with surge rates, pay via UPI/Cash, and present your PIN.
